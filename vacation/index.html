Welcome to Budget Vacations

Budget Vacations is more than a travel agency, it is your new path to serenity and peace, guiding you on an unforgettable journey to rejuvenate your soul. A well-planned vacation has the power to heal and inspire. Budget Vacations specializes in crafting personalized vacation packages that allow clients to leave their stresses behind and embrace the world’s most beautiful and serene destinations. Whether you dream of a sunny beach or a mountain retreat, Budget Vacations ensures every detail is thoughtfully managed, allowing you to immerse yourself in the moment. 

Budget Vacation's mission is simple: to help clients find their inner peace by taking care of every aspect of their travel plans. From selecting the ideal destination to booking itineraries and providing ongoing support throughout the journey, the agency provides an environment of calm and confidence. Clients trust that they are in expert hands, free to enjoy a journey suited to their needs and wants.

Our Story

Budget Vacations was founded by Daniel Budget, a man who had a lifelong passion for exploration and a deep appreciation for the power of travel. After years of planning his own peaceful getaways, he realized there was a gap in the market for a travel agency focused on creating truly peaceful travel experiences. In 2015, Budget turned his vision into reality.  What began as a small operation catering to friends and family quickly grew into a trusted name in the travel industry. Budget built his reputation on a foundation of care, attention to detail, and a commitment to client satisfaction. Today, Budget Vacations continues to uphold the founders’ original vision, helping travelers find serenity in every corner of the globe.

Popular Destinations

  • Santorini, Greece: Explore ancient ruins, enjoy local wines at a cliffside vineyard, and relax on the unique black and red sand beaches.
  • Kyoto, Japan: Attend a traditional tea ceremony, walk through the famous Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, and experience authentic Japanese food.
  • Banff National Park, Canada: Hike around turquoise lakes like Lake Louise, enjoy natural hot springs, and ride scenic gondolas.
  • Amalfi Coast, Italy: Explore the streets of Positano, enjoy a boat ride to Capri, and feast on authentic Italian gelato.
