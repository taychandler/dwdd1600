Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get good at the piano?
In my experience, it takes several years. I started lessons when I was 5 years old and kept taking lessons until I was 16. I practiced nearly everyday for hours. I practiced because I loved it but also because I knew that is how I would master playing the piano.
Do I need to take lessons?
You don't have to do anything. I would highly recommend taking lessons either with a teacher or through YouTube videos or online master classes. Learning piano technique and music theory is highly beneficial to your ability to read music and learn the notes effectively on the keyboard.
Does playing the piano require natural talent?
No. I know several people who are practically tone deaf, yet they are able to pluck along on the piano. The piano, once you learn technique and how to move along the keyboard is almost pattern based. If you are able to pick up on patterns, you will probably do fine.
